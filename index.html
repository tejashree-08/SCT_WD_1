Frequently asked questions

Is SkillBridge free for students?+

Yes! Core features including internship discovery and roadmaps are free for all students.

How are mentors verified?+

Every mentor goes through a rigorous verification process based on industry experience.

Do I get a certificate after assessments?+

Absolutely. You earn verifiable, shareable certificates on completing skill assessments.

Can companies post internships here?+

Yes. Companies can sign up and post unlimited internship listings.

How do I become a mentor?+

Click "Become a Mentor" in the hero section and fill in your profile.
